Haus Gables – Cross‑Laminated Timber Residence

Precision assembly of a complex multi‑gable CLT structure. Our team coordinated crane operations, panel sequencing, and on‑site QA to deliver a clean, rapid install.

Project Overview

Designed by Jennifer Bonner/MALL in Atlanta, GA, Haus Gables demonstrates the possibilities of mass timber in residential construction.

  • System: Cross‑Laminated Timber (CLT)
  • Scope: Structural panel placement & coordination
  • Highlights: Complex geometry, rapid assembly, clean finish
