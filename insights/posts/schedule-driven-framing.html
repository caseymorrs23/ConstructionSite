Schedule‑Driven Framing: Handoffs that Hold

GQC delivers schedule‑aligned framing with a repeatable approach: daily coordination with the GC and trades, clean scope boundaries, and quality checks that happen early—when they are fast to fix.

On every build we focus on: layout precision, shear and hold‑down checks, WRB tie‑ins, window and door integration, and early weather protection. Crews move efficiently because staging is organized, and handoffs are planned with the site superintendent so tasks don’t collide.

For CLT, our rigging plans and panel sequencing reduce crane time and rework. Envelope work follows details straight from the drawings—backed by our checklists—so performance is built in, not inspected in.

That’s how we reduce punchlists, avoid call‑backs, and keep cost and schedule risk off the critical path.
