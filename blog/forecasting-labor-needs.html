Forecasting Labor Needs: Scaling Crews for Large‑Scale Wood Framing Projects

Insights for GCs pricing large, schedule-driven work.

Outputs that Convert to Durations

Forecasting starts with outputs—units framed per day, walls set per hour, stairs per shift. We adjust for shear density, floor height, material handling, and weather windows. Those outputs roll up into durations that owners and lenders can trust.

With predictable durations, we can size crews to hit milestones without waste. It’s how we keep manpower steady and morale high.

Flexing Without Chaos

When the plan changes, we flex crews purposefully: a recovery squad to a critical zone, not a full team shuffle. This protects productivity and keeps safety controls intact.
